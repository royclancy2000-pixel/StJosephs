Admissions · Grades 8–12

Is your son called here?

We are looking for boys of good will — not finished products, but young men ready to be formed in faith, work, and the confidence to build.

Who It's For

The boy we hope to form

St. Joseph's Schola is for families who want more for their sons than a credential — who want them holy, capable, and ready to take responsibility for a life and a livelihood.

Our students come from faithful Catholic families seeking serious formation paired with real-world competence. A boy need not arrive a scholar, an entrepreneur, or a saint; he needs to arrive willing — willing to pray, to work hard, to live in community, to be corrected, and to grow. We will meet that willingness with everything we have.

The Process

Four steps to enrollment

Step 1

Inquire

Complete the inquiry form below. We will follow up personally to answer your questions and learn about your son.

Step 2

Visit

Come to campus for a tour and a day in the life. We strongly encourage every family to see the school before applying.

Step 3

Apply

Submit the formal application, records, and a short essay from your son. A family interview follows.

Step 4

Enroll

Upon acceptance, secure your son's place and begin the journey. We walk with you every step of the way.

Requirements

What we ask

  • Grades 8 through 12. Boys entering these grades are eligible to apply.
  • Academic records. Transcripts or records from the previous two years of schooling.
  • A student essay. A short, handwritten letter from your son on why he wishes to come.
  • A pastoral reference. A letter from your parish priest or a trusted reference.
  • A family interview. A conversation with the headmaster, in person or by video.
  • A willing heart. The one requirement we cannot waive.
